Why choose a broker

Finance Brokers vs. Lenders

There's a real difference between going direct and working with a broker. Here's why thousands of Australians choose us first.

Finance Brokers

  • Multiple loan options from our entire lender panel
  • Expert assistance with complex or unique situations
  • We negotiate between you and the lender on your behalf
  • Knowledge of diverse loan products across the market
  • Pre-approval assessments without hard credit checks
  • Compare features like offset accounts, redraw, and rates

Going Direct to a Lender

  • Only their own products and rates — no comparisons
  • Limited flexibility for unique or complex circumstances
  • No independent negotiation on your behalf
  • Standard lending criteria only — no tailored approach
  • Each application may impact your credit score
  • You do all the research and comparison yourself
