## GA Finance & Loan Studio Join Forces to Create Melbourne's Premier Mortgage Brokerage

Two established mortgage broking firms have announced their merger, combining resources and expertise. The announcement emphasizes their shared dedication to helping Australians achieve homeownership goals.

## A Brief History of Loan Studio

Founded in 2011, the original firm built its reputation on transparent solutions for homebuyers, investors, and refinancers. The company earned recognition including nominations for the Australian Mortgage Awards and accolades for service excellence.

## The Legacy of GA Finance

GA Finance established itself through personalized guidance and market expertise in complex lending scenarios. The team received nominations for multiple industry awards, recognizing their innovative lending approach and client satisfaction focus.

## A Bigger, Stronger Team

The combined entity now employs more than 30 professionals, expanding from 10 dedicated team members. This growth enables faster processing, broader lending options, and enhanced capacity for diverse client needs.

## Why This Matters for Clients

The merger delivers several advantages:

- Expanded access to bank and non-bank lenders
- Increased combined industry knowledge
- Larger, more specialized support team
- Continued focus on customized solutions

## Looking Ahead

The unified organization maintains its core mission while leveraging enhanced capabilities. The firm positions itself to serve first-time buyers, refinancers, and investment portfolio builders across Australia with award-caliber service.
