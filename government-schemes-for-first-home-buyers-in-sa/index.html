Government Schemes for First Home Buyers in South Australia

If you're a first home buyer in South Australia, we're here to help! Explore the grants, concessions, and schemes available to make your home ownership dreams a reality.

First Home Owner Grant (FHOG)

$15,000 Grant

South Australia offers a $15,000 grant to eligible first home buyers purchasing or building a new home. This grant helps reduce the upfront costs of entering the property market.

Eligibility Requirements:

  • Must be purchasing or building a new home valued under $650,000
  • Must be an Australian citizen or permanent resident
  • Must live in the property as your primary residence
  • Must not have previously owned property in Australia

Stamp Duty Exemption

South Australia offers a full stamp duty exemption for eligible first home buyers purchasing new homes, providing significant savings on upfront costs.
