The Process

How it works

Five steps from where you are now to the front door of your next home.

1

Decide your next move

Address whether to sell or rent your current property to determine the best financing options for your next purchase. This key decision shapes your entire strategy.

2

Understand your borrowing options

Calculate your borrowing capacity and explore how to tap into existing equity from your current property. Understanding your position gives you confidence when making offers.

3

Get pre-approval on your finance

Secure pre-approval to strengthen your property offers and give sellers confidence in your ability to complete the purchase. Pre-approval is your competitive edge.

4

Preparing the application

Submit your formal loan application through our local team's guidance. We handle the documentation and liaise with lenders on your behalf throughout the process.

5

Complete purchase and move in

Finalise your loan details and coordinate settlement. We work alongside your conveyancer to make sure everything lines up smoothly so you can focus on the exciting part — moving in.
